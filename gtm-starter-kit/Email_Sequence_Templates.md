# 5-Touch Email Sequence Templates
**Fill in the [brackets]. Send in order. Follow the schedule.**

---

## How to Use These Templates

1. Pick the segment that matches your prospect (A-E below)
2. Replace every `[bracket]` with your specific details
3. Follow the send schedule: Day 0, 3, 8, 14, 22
4. T1-T3 go in the SAME email thread (reply to yourself)
5. T4 and T5 are NEW threads with new subject lines

---

## Segment A: High-Urgency Buyers
**Use when:** The prospect has a financial or contractual reason to care about your product. Think: guarantees, SLAs, penalty clauses, refund policies.

### T1 — Cold Open
**Subject:** `[company] and [your topic]`

> Hi [Name],
>
> [Company]'s [guarantee/SLA/commitment] is a strong promise — and [the stage where your product matters] is where it gets tested hardest.
>
> I run [Your Company], [one sentence: what you do]. We [specific deliverable] — [why it matters in their context].
>
> [One sentence tying your product to their financial risk].
>
> Worth 20 minutes this week?
>
> [Your name]
> [Your Company] | [website]
> Book a call: [calendly]

### T2 — Value Proof (Day 3)
**Subject:** Re: [same as T1]

> Hi [Name],
>
> One thing we see with [type of company]: [specific insight about their world — not about you].
>
> [One sentence connecting that insight to what you do].
>
> Happy to walk through this in a quick call.
>
> [Your name]

### T3 — Angle Flip (Day 8)
**Subject:** Re: [same as T1]

> Hi [Name],
>
> Different angle — [flip the perspective. If T1 was about their business risk, this is about their end user/customer/employee].
>
> [One sentence with a new reason to care].
>
> Open to 20 minutes?
>
> [Your name]

### T4 — Insight Drop (Day 14)
**Subject:** `[industry-level insight headline]`

> Hi [Name],
>
> [Share something you've learned from working in this space — a pattern, a stat, a shift you're seeing. No pitch.]
>
> [Connect it to a broader trend].
>
> [Optional: mention how your work touches this — but keep it light].
>
> [Your name]

### T5 — Breakup (Day 22)
**Subject:** `closing the loop`

> Hi [Name],
>
> Didn't want to keep chasing if the timing's off or it's not a fit.
>
> Three options — reply with a number:
>
> 1. Interested — let's find 20 minutes this week
> 2. Not now — happy to check back in a few months
> 3. Not a fit — good to know either way
>
> [Your name]

## Segment B: Corporate / Enterprise Pathway
**Use when:** The prospect places people into corporate roles. Their reputation depends on placement quality.

### T1
**Subject:** `[company] and [placement quality topic]`

> Hi [Name],
>
> [Company] [people/graduates/candidates] can [do the core skill] — but [the real-world test] is a different format entirely.
>
> I run [Your Company], [one sentence]. We [what you do] — the specific prep that matters when [their client/employer] is making a decision.
>
> Strong [outcomes] protect the [client/employer] relationship. Weak ones damage it.
>
> Worth 20 minutes?
>
> [Your name]
> [Your Company] | [website]

### T2 (Day 3)
**Subject:** Re: [same as T1]

> Hi [Name],
>
> We've noticed [specific pattern about their type of client/employer] — [what that means for preparation/quality].
>
> [One sentence on how you address this].
>
> Worth a quick conversation?
>
> [Your name]

### T3 (Day 8)
**Subject:** Re: [same as T1]

> Hi [Name],
>
> [Flip to the candidate/user perspective — what they experience, not what the company needs].
>
> [Why your product helps from that angle].
>
> [Your name]

>

>
> Worth 20 minutes?
>
> [Your name]

## Segment D: Career Changers / Returners

>

>
> [Your name]

## Segment E: Large / Institutional Buyers
**Use when:** Universities, government programmes, large nonprofits. Longer cycles, stakeholder complexity.

### T1
**Subject:** `[topic] at [institution]`

> Hi [Name],
>
> Most [institution] [audience] receive strong [existing support] but very little structured [what you provide].
>
> I run [Your Company] — [one sentence]. We work with [buyer type] to give [audience] the [specific outcome] they need before [their next milestone].
>
> [Relevant metric] is something I imagine you watch closely.
>
> Worth 20 minutes?
>
> [Your name]

*(T2-T5 — adapt from Segment A)*
