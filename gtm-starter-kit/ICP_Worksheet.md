## Part 4: Pain Signal Checklist

Check which signals apply to your market. These tell you what angle to lead with in emails:

- [ ] They're hiring for a role your product replaces or supports
- [ ] Recent funding (new budget, growth pressure)
- [ ] Competitor launched something in your space
- [ ] Negative reviews mentioning your problem area
- [ ] New leadership in the buyer role
- [ ] Seasonal peak approaching
- [ ] Regulatory change affecting their industry
- [ ] Public commitment (guarantee, SLA) tied to your area
- [ ] Job postings for a role that solves the problem you solve
