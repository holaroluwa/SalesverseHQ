## Subject Line Formulas

Use these as starting points. Replace the brackets:

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| `[company] and [topic]` | `stripe and developer hiring` |
| `[topic] for [company] [audience]` | `interview prep for acme grads` |
| `[company]'s [model] and [your area]` | `acme's guarantee and pass rates` |
| `[audience] at [company]` | `junior devs at acme` |
| `[topic] at [company]` | `onboarding speed at acme` |

**For T4 (insight email):**

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| `what [audience] actually [do]` | `what hiring managers actually test` |
| `the [problem] in [industry]` | `the gap in bootcamp hiring` |
| `[number] things [audience] miss` | `3 things candidates miss in finals` |

**For T5 (breakup):**
Always: `closing the loop`
